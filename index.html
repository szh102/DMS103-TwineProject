DaySim



This is a college student daily routine simulation game.
There are many things you can't control in your life. So this simulation game consist of many randomized variables.
Therefore, most of the stories are based on random events that the system generate, but these event will affect your mood.
At the end of the day, you'll write a diary, that will reflect your mood score of the day.
If the score is higher than 5, you have a generally good day.
If the score is lower than 5, you had a bad day, sorry about that, but that's life...
